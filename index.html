Grilled Chicken with Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Grill the chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  4. Toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Grill the vegetables for 3-4 minutes, until tender.
  6. Serve the grilled chicken with the vegetables on the side.

Besan ka Chilla

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the gram flour, water, chopped onion, green chili, turmeric powder, salt, and cilantro to make a smooth batter.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it with oil.
  3. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread it evenly.
  4. Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with yogurt or chutney.

Oats Omelet

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the oats, eggs, milk, onion, tomato, green chili, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and add a little oil.
  3. Pour the mixture into the pan and spread it evenly.
  4. Cook on medium heat for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with a side of salad.

Aloo Tikki Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 slices of whole wheat bread
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the mashed potatoes, onion, green chili, cumin powder, and salt.
  2. Form the mixture into small patties (tikkis).
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and add a little butter or oil.
  4. Cook the tikkis on medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Place the tikkis between slices of whole wheat bread to make sandwiches.
  6. Grill the sandwiches until crispy and serve hot.

Beetroot Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium beetroots, boiled and diced
  • 1 small apple, diced
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the diced beetroots, apple, walnuts, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve chilled.
